What is Cold2Close and how does it work? +

Cold2Close is an AI-powered cold calling solution that transforms your outbound sales process. We create custom workflows for any business where our AI makes calls, engages prospects in natural conversations, and generates qualified leads for your sales team.

How do I get started with Cold2Close? +

Getting started is simple! Just fill out our Get Started form with your business details and requirements. Our team will contact you to discuss your specific needs and create a custom workflow tailored to your business.

What types of businesses can benefit from Cold2Close? +

We work with businesses across various industries including real estate, SaaS, healthcare, financial services, marketing agencies, and more. Any business that relies on outbound calling can benefit from our AI-powered solution.

How much does Cold2Close cost? +

We offer several pricing tiers starting at $99/month for our Starter plan, $299/month for Professional, and $599/month for our Build Me plan. Each plan includes different features and call volumes. Custom solutions are also available.

Can I customize the AI calling scripts? +

Absolutely! Our Professional and Build Me plans include custom scripting. We work closely with you to develop conversation flows that match your brand voice, target audience, and specific business objectives.

How do you handle data privacy and security? +

Data privacy and security are our top priorities. We implement industry-standard security measures, comply with relevant regulations, and never sell your data. For detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy.

What integrations do you offer? +

We offer seamless integration with popular CRMs and marketing platforms. Our system can sync data with your existing tools to automate follow-up workflows and ensure smooth data transfer between systems.

How do you measure success and ROI? +

We provide detailed analytics on call performance, lead quality, conversion rates, and ROI. Our dashboard gives you insights into which conversations are most effective and how to optimize your campaigns for better results.

What kind of support do you provide? +

All plans include email support, with priority support for Professional and Build Me plans. Our Build Me plan includes a dedicated account manager and 24/7 premium support to ensure your success.

Can I try the AI before committing? +

Yes! We offer a "Talk to AI" demo where you can experience a real conversation with our AI assistant. This gives you a firsthand look at how our technology works before making any commitment.
